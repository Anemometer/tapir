Thank you sincerely for your support on Startnext. We are thrilled to welcome you as a member of our cooperative, SuperCoop Berlin eG.

To complete your membership, please…

  1. Review our bylaws and let us know if you have any questions.
  2. Review the attached registration form to make sure your personal information is correct.
  3. Print, add your date of birth, and sign the form. If you don't have access to a printer, come see us at Osram Höfe (see below).
  4. You have 3 options for returning your signed registration form:
    1. Visit us at our current storage location in Osram Höfe (Oudenarder Str. 16 - see map). We are there every Wednesday from 14:00 to 20:00 and every Saturday from 10:00 to 14:00.
    2. Mail it by post to: SuperCoop Berlin eG, Glogauer Straße 21, 10999 Berlin
    3. Email a scanned copy with your original signature to mitglied@supercoop.de (digital signatures are not possible at this point).

As soon as we have received your completed and signed document, we will send you an official confirmation of membership via email. :){% if not u.is_investing %}With confirmation of your membership, you will receive all information on how to sign up for a shift, shop and vote at SuperCoop.{% endif %}
